[h3] Supported versions [/h3]
V65 - V71

[h3] Description [/h3]
For V67 and higher, this mod gives archery range two upgrades similar to barracks, each upgrade giving a 50% speed boost to training.
The upgrades cost bows and metal based on the size of the archery range.
On V65 & V66, this mod added some research nodes that were later integrated to the vanilla game on V70.

[h3] Compatibility [/h3]
This mod modifies the vanilla files init/room/archery_vanilla.
if you are using a mod that touches the same files, depending on the load order, one mod may overwrite the conflicting files of the other mod.
Since V71, instead of modifying whole vanilla files, its now possible to modify only specific areas of the files, improving compatibility.
Should be safe to add/remove to a save, do a backup first just in case! Your allocated knowledge points may reset after adding the mod.

[h3] Notes [/h3]
Archery range's sprites dont "upgrade" when upgrading the room, but the extra speed is still obtained.
