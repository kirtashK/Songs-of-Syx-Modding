Note! Some of the infographics above are for V70 and are outdated for V71 (dont have time to change it at the moment), some stats have changed:
Crossbows are weaker, closer to bows. They might be too weak now, or still be strong, feedback is welcome, barelly had time to test.
Pikes' frontal defenses are all 2x.
Metal spears are one handed and can be used with mounts unlike vanilla spear. All frontal defenses are 1.5x. Charge is increased by 1.25x.
Heavy shield's frontal defenses for slash and pierce are 2x, blunt remains 6x. Offense skill is reduced by 15% instead of 5%. Movement speed is reduced by 15% instead of 10%.
Whetstone gives a multiplicative bonus (10%) instead of additive (1 damage). This means weapons that only do pierce damage dont deal extra slash damage.
Stone arrows' boost is 0.2. Metal arrows' boost is 0.5.

All these items are made at a new workshop, Armory.
There are technology nodes to unlock the armory, boosts & upgrades to the armory, aswell as recipes and crossbow training speed boosts.
To improve crossbow skill you can use a shooting range, similar to bow training. Crossbow training is faster but a fully trained crossbowmen doesnt improve as much, mostly reload and accuracy.

[h3] What about AI factions? [/h3]
The mod adds new divisions along with the vanilla ones in order to allow AI to use these items.
For example, you may find a division carrying plate armor, heavy shield and a morning star, as well as a division with armor leather, vanilla shield and dagger.
In total, this mod adds 50 options for divisions, 16 of them being vanilla options carrying banners, whetsones, arrows or heavy shields.

[h3] Compatibility [/h3]
Doesnt touch vanilla files so it should be compatible with other mods.
Requires a new save, cant be removed from a save.
